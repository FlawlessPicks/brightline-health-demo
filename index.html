Brightline Health Collectibles

A Secure Innovation Powered by ChatGPT & Firebase

Invented by Joel Lawless
This project showcases a unique approach to verified digital collectibles designed for health institutions. Each collectible can represent medical achievements, treatment proof, or patient rewards — all backed by secure blockchain-style tracking and protected by OpenAI’s ChatGPT documentation.

This demo serves as a formal timestamp and digital proof of concept for the invention known as "Flawless Collectibles", extended to the health industry.
